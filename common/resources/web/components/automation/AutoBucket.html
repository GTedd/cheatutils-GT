Automatically uses water bucket when you fall.
Module only searches water bucket on your hotbar.
Don't rely solely on this module, since you can die just because of bad ping.
Don't fall on waterloggable blocks like leaves, since your water cannot be placed on top of such blocks.
Module doesn't check if you already are falling into water.
There was plans to add other items like cobweb or slime block.
Speed Threshold
Module tries to use water bucket only if your speed (blocks/sec) if greater than specified value.
Items to Use
Automatically picks up water once you land.
Reach Distance
Module tries to place water if distance to target block less than this distance. Default Minecraft reach distance is 4.5 and it is not recommended to increase it, since even vanilla server will just reject water place actions on longer distances.
