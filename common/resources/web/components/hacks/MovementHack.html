Different hacks related to movement.
No slow on item use
Don't reduce your speed when you use consumable items, such as food or potions.
Override max speed
Max speed factor:
Value 1 means default speed. Value 2 increase max speed by 2 and so on.
No slow on crouch
Don't reduce your speed when you are crouching or crawling.
Anti-Knockback
Ignores server packet responsible for setting your speed. Knockback from mobs or players will be ignored.
Anti-Push
Skips client-side code responsible for moving your character based on nearby entities.
Disable Water Push Physics
Water no longer moves your character.
Override jump height
Jump height factor:
Value 1 means default jump height. Value 2 increase jump height by 2 and so on.
Disable Slime Block Physics
No slow, no bounces.
